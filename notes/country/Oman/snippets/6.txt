Nakhal Fort
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nakhal_Fort
<small>[from en.wikipedia.org]:</small> Nakhal Fort is a large fortification in Al Batinah Region of Oman. It is named after the Wilayah of Nakhal. The fort houses a museum, operated by the Ministry of Tourism, which has exhibits of historic guns, and the fort also hosts a weekly goat market.
