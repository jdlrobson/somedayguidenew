<small>[from en.wikipedia.org]:</small> The crested quetzal /ketSAHL/ is a species of bird in the family Trogonidae native to South America. The best places to see the Resplendent Quetzal include Monteverde Cloud Forest Reserve in Costa Rica between February and July.
