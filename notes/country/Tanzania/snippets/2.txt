<small>[from en.wikipedia.org]:</small> The Serengeti National Park is a large national park in northern Tanzania that stretches over 14,763 km2 (5,700 sq mi). It is located in eastern Mara Region and northeastern Simiyu Region and contains over 1.5 million ha of virgin savanna. The park was established in 1940.
