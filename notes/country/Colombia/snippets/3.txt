Cali Fair
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cali_Fair
<small>[from en.wikipedia.org]:</small> Cali Fair is a cultural event held in Cali, Colombia from December 25 to December 30. It is a celebration of the region's cultural identity, famous for the Salsa marathon, horse riding parades and dance parties.
