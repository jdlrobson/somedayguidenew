Palais des congrès de Montréal
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Palais_des_congr%C3%A8s_de_Montr%C3%A9al
<small>[from en.wikipedia.org]:</small> The Palais des congrès de Montréal is a convention centre in Montreal's Quartier international at the north end of Old Montreal. Its borough is Ville-Marie. Construction began in 1977 and completed in 1983; the Palais opened on 21 May 1983. Victor Prus designed the original building.
