Djémila
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dj%C3%A9mila
<small>[from en.wikipedia.org]:</small> Djémila, formerly Cuicul, is a small mountain village in Algeria, near the northern coast east of Algiers, where some of the best preserved Roman ruins in North Africa are found. It is situated in the region bordering the Constantinois and Petite Kabylie.
