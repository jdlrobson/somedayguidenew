Dunajec River Gorge
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dunajec_River_Gorge
<small>[from en.wikipedia.org]:</small> The Dunajec River Gorge runs through the Pieniny Mountains in the south of Poland and the north of Slovakia. The gorge is characterized by some of the most interesting geological and geomorphological structures and area-specific natural ecosystems with little anthropogenic influence. It is featured on UNESCO's Tentative List of World Heritage Sites in Poland.
