Bonassola, the eighth Cinque Terre ;)
https://thatsliguria.com/en/bonassola-the-eighth-cinque-terre/
Bonassola is a small, calm town located in East Liguria, just next to Levanto and very, very close to Cinque Terre. Has a fantastic beach and offers an amazing possibility to enjoy your holiday in the most touristic part of Liguria but without crowds! <small>[image from That's Liguria!]</small>
