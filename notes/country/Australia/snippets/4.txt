Quokka
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Quokka
<small>[from en.wikipedia.org]:</small> The quokka is a small macropod about the size of a domestic cat. It is the only member of the genus Setonix. Like other marsupials in the macropod family, the quokka is herbivorous and mainly nocturnal.
