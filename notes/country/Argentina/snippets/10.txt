Penitente (snow formation)
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Penitente_(snow_formation)
<small>[from en.wikipedia.org]:</small> Penitentes, or nieves penitentes, are snow formations found at high altitudes. They take the form of elongated, thin blades of hardened snow or ice, closely spaced and pointing towards the general direction of the sun.
