Hwange National Park
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hwange_National_Park
<small>[from en.wikipedia.org]:</small> Hwange National Park is the largest natural reserve in Zimbabwe. It is around 14,600 sq km in area. It lies in the northwest of the country, just off the main road between Bulawayo and Victoria Falls. The nearest town is Dete.
