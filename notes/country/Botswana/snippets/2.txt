<small>[from en.wikipedia.org]:</small> The Okavango Delta in Botswana is a vast inland delta formed where the Okavango River reaches a tectonic trough at an altitude of 930–1,000 metres (3,050–3,280 ft) in the central part of the endorheic basin of the Kalahari Desert.
