Sela Cetiya
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sela_Cetiya
<small>[from en.wikipedia.org]:</small> The Sela Cetiya, also known as the Ambastala Dagaba, is a significant stupa located at the Mihintale Royal Rock Temple in Sri Lanka. It is one of five stupas within the temple premises and is considered one of the 16 main places of worship, or Solosmasthana.
