<small>[from en.wikipedia.org]:</small> Water Towns, also called Canal Towns, are certain ancient and historic towns in China known for their bridges, rivers, and canals. Such towns exist in many regions in China, although those in Jiangsu and Zhejiang provinces are often the most renowned.
