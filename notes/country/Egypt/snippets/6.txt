Valley of the Kings
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Valley_of_the_Kings
<small>[from en.wikipedia.org]:</small> The Valley of the Kings, also known as the Valley of the Gates of the Kings, is an area in Egypt where, for a period of nearly 500 years from the Eighteenth Dynasty to the Twentieth Dynasty, rock-cut tombs were excavated for pharaohs and powerful nobles under the New Kingdom of ancient Egypt.
