https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Victoria_Falls
<small>[from en.wikipedia.org]:</small> Victoria Falls is a waterfall on the Zambezi River, located on the border between Zambia and Zimbabwe. It is one of the world's largest waterfalls, with a width of 1,708 m (5,604 ft). The region around it has a high degree of biodiversity in both plants and animals.
