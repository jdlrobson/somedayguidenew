Kathmandu Durbar Square
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kathmandu_Durbar_Square
<small>[from en.wikipedia.org]:</small> Kathmandu Durbar Square is a historically and culturally significant site in Kathmandu, Nepal. It is one of the three Durbar Squares in the Kathmandu Valley in Nepal that are UNESCO World Heritage Sites.
