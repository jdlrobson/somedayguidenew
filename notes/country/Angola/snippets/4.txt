Black Rocks at Pungo Andongo
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Black_Rocks_at_Pungo_Andongo
<small>[from en.wikipedia.org]:</small> The Black Rocks at Pungo Andongo are a set of extensive monolithic rock formations in Angola. Millions of years old, they stand out for their size in relation to the savanna landscape of the region. It is subdivided into Western, South, North and Southeast subsystems. The formation is an extension of the Cacuso Plateau.
