Seven Sisters Waterfall (Norway)
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Seven_Sisters_Waterfall_(Norway)
<small>[from en.wikipedia.org]:</small> The Seven Sisters is the 39th tallest waterfall in Norway. The 410-metre (1,350 ft) tall waterfall consists of seven separate streams, and the tallest of the seven has a free fall that measures 250 metres (820 ft).
