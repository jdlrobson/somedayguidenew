Kiwi (bird)
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kiwi_(bird)
<small>[from en.wikipedia.org]:</small> Kiwi are flightless birds endemic to New Zealand of the order Apterygiformes. The five extant species fall into the family Apterygidae and genus Apteryx. Approximately the size of a domestic chicken, kiwi are the smallest ratites.
