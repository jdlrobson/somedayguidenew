El Perú (Maya site)
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/El_Per%C3%BA_(Maya_site)
<small>[from en.wikipedia.org]:</small> El Perú, is a pre-Columbian Maya archeological site occupied during the Preclassic and Classic cultural chronology periods. The site was the capital of a Maya city-state and is located near the banks of the San Pedro River in the Department of Petén of northern Guatemala. El Perú is 60 km (37 mi) west of Tikal.
