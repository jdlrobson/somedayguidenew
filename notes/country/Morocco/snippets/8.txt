Fez Festival of World Sacred Music
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fez_Festival_of_World_Sacred_Music
<small>[from en.wikipedia.org]:</small> The Fez Festival of World Sacred Music is an annual music festival that is held for a week in Fez, Morocco. It was first held in 1994 and usually held over 10 days in early June. The festival includes concerts, debates, and joint performances of Muslim and Christian devotional music from artists from all over the world.
