Mellieħa
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mellie%C4%A7a
<small>[from en.wikipedia.org]:</small> Mellieħa is a large village in the Northern Region of Malta. It has a population of 10,087 as of March 2014. Mellieħa is also a tourist resort, popular for its sandy beaches, natural environment, and Popeye Village nearby.
