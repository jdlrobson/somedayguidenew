Fårö
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/F%C3%A5r%C3%B6
<small>[from en.wikipedia.org]:</small> Fårö or Fåre in Gutnish is a Baltic Sea island just north of the island of Gotland, itself off mainland Sweden's southeastern coast. It is the second-largest island in the county and it is a popular summer resort. It has its own language, Faroymal, a dialect of Gutnish.
