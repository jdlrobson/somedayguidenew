Dead Sea
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dead_Sea
<small>[from en.wikipedia.org]:</small> The Dead Sea, also known by other names, is a landlocked salt lake bordered by Jordan to the east, the Israeli-occupied West Bank to the west and Israel to the southwest. It lies in the endorheic basin of the Jordan Rift Valley, and its main tributary is the Jordan River.
