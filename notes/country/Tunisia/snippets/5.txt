Hammamet, Tunisia
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hammamet,_Tunisia
<small>[from en.wikipedia.org]:</small> Hammamet is a town in the Nabeul Governorate of Tunisia. Due to its beaches, it is a popular destination for swimming and water sports and is one of the primary tourist destinations in Tunisia. It is located in the south-eastern section of Cape Bon.
