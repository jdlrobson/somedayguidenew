Boryeong Mud Festival
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Boryeong_Mud_Festival
<small>[from en.wikipedia.org]:</small> The Boryeong Mud Festival (Korean: 보령머드축제) is an annual festival which takes place during the summer on Daecheon Beach (대천해수욕장) near Boryeong, a town some 200 km south of Seoul, South Korea. The first Mud Festival was staged in 1998 and, by 2007, the festival attracted 2.2 million visitors to Boryeong.
